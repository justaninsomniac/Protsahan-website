About Us

Protsahan is the inimitable product of NMIMS's unique lust for life and it's proverbial tendency towards excellence. As a result, it is a 4-day extravaganza with equal parts of awesomeness in organisation and turnout. Protsahan has its own unmistakable voice that is reflected in the way we connect to people and problems through publicity or social campaigns. Protsahan aspires to be the melting pot of nation's liveliest to create the ultimate uplifting ambiance of harmony, happiness and creation.
